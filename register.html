After you register: if you need a detailed invoice, then fill this form. If you need a letter of invitation for your visa application, then fill this form (you must be registered).

An author from each paper is required to register and attend the conference. If there are exceptional circumstances that prohibit you from attending, then please email comms@rl-conference.cc

If you are a recently graduated student publishing under your student affilitation, then you should register as a Student. For other non-industry researchers, including postdocs, faculty and independent researchers, register as an Academic.

Cancellation Policy

Registrations canceled before July 14, 2026 will receive a full refund. Refunds will be issued back to your credit card and may take up to 10 business days to appear on your statement.

In the special case of a visa issues registrations can be cancelled for a full refund up to the conference start date. You must apply for the visa before June 1st, 2026 and show proof of visa refusal or proof of application withdrawal to be eligible. To request a refund, email refunds@rl-conference.cc with the subject "RLC Refund".
