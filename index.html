The second Reinforcement Learning Conference (RLC) will take place from August 5th to 8th, 2025, at the University of Alberta, Edmonton.

About RLC

RLC is an annual international conference focusing on reinforcement learning. It provides an archival venue where reinforcement learning researchers can interact and share their research in a more focused setting than typical large machine learning venues. The RLC peer review process prioritizes rigorous methodology over perceived importance, aiming to foster scholarly discussions on both well-established and emerging topics in RL. Papers accepted at the conference are published as articles in the Reinforcement Learning Journal (RLJ).
