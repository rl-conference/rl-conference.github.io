About RLC

RLC provides a venue where reinforcement learning researchers can interact and share their research in a more focused setting than typical large machine learning venues. All work presented at RLC is published in the associated Reinforcement Learning Journal (RLJ). The RLJ is an independent, peer-reviewed journal that serves as a primary source of scholarly articles. The RLC enhances the reach and impact of the research published within the journal by allowing authors to present and elaborate on their findings that are published in RLJ.
