Banquet Details

The RLC banquet dinner will be held on Wednesday, August 6th (6pm) at Edmonton Convention Centre in Hall D. Doors open at 6pm, with the buffet dinner served at 6:45 pm. Fun entertainment is planned with Improv by RapidFire Theatre and a Puzzle Hunt by Michael Bowling and Michael Littman.

Dress code: Your normal conference attire. Or whatever you feel most comfortable in for a buffet meal and evening of light entertainment and good conversation.

Address: 9797 Jasper Ave, Edmonton, AB T5J 1N9

Venue info & detailed instructions: Getting Here

Directions by train from campus

  1. From University station, take a north-bound train to Central station.
  2. Exit to street level from the platform.
  3. Walk east along Jasper Ave (~5 minutes) to the Edmonton Convention Centre.

See maps below.

Directions by walking from campus

  1. Walk east from the conference venue to 109 St.
  2. Walk north on 109 St. across the river via the High Level Bridge (pedestrian/bike trail) until you reach Jasper Avenue (or detour through the legislature grounds).
  3. Head east along Jasper Avenue to the banquet address.

This walk takes around 1 hour.
