Main Information

Airport transportation: The Route 747 bus runs from the airport to Century Park station, from which you can transfer and take the LRT to University station. There are also ride-sharing apps, such as Uber, which can take you to and from the airport and city.

Conference venue: RLC 2025 will be held at the University of Alberta. Note that registration will also provide a transit pass for local city transportation.

Banquet: A banquet dinner will be held on Wednesday August 7th (6pm) at Edmonton Convention Centre. From University station, take a north-bound train to Central station. From there, it is a ~5 minute walk west along Jasper Ave.

Venue Details

Location and directions: The conference will be held in The Centennial Centre for Interdisciplinary Science (CCIS). See below for directions from University station to CCIS.

Poster printing: SUBPrint can provide poster printing service, and it is located on the south-west corner of the main quad (see the map below). We suggest that you place an order for your printing ahead of time, due to increased demand.

City transportation: Public transportation, via Edmonton Transport Service (ETS), is available to the University of Alberta campus. University station is connected to the city by light-rail transit (LRT), as well as by bus. Common bus routes to and from the university include the 4 (going east-west) and the 8 (going north-south).

Wi-Fi access: There are two WiFi networks for use on the University of Alberta campus: Eduroam and UofA-Guest. Note that Eduroam requires university login credentials from your home institution.

Food Options

Conference-provided: Lunch will be provided from Wednesday to Friday.

On campus: Student Union Building and HUB Mall both have fast-food options.

Near campus (<20 min walk): Farrow Garneau, Sugarbowl, Mukja Korean Street Food, Osmow's Shawarma, Nosh Cafe.

Further from campus (30+ min walk, ~10 min bus): There are many restaurants along 82 ave, also known as Whyte ave.

Other Local Information

Weather expectations: Expect sunny days, cool nights and a dry climate.

Explore Edmonton: Local resource for festivals and events in the city

Edmonton Unlimited: Local tech ecosystem and innovation hub.

Attractions accessible by transit: Royal Alberta Museum, Art Gallery of Alberta, Muttart Conservatory

Attractions requiring car transport: Fort Edmonton Park, University of Alberta Botanic Garden, Elk Island National Park
